Refund & Return Policy

Last updated: January 2024

1. Return Eligibility

You may return most new, unopened items within 7 days of delivery for a full refund. Items must be:

  • In original packaging
  • Unused and in the same condition as received
  • With all tags and labels attached
  • With proof of purchase

2. Non-Returnable Items

Certain items cannot be returned:

  • Items that have been used or installed
  • Products with missing packaging or accessories
  • Damaged items not reported within 48 hours of delivery
  • Personalized or custom-made products

3. Return Process

To initiate a return:

  1. Contact our customer service within 7 days of receiving your order
  2. Provide your order number and reason for return
  3. Wait for return authorization and shipping instructions
  4. Pack the item securely in original packaging
  5. Ship the item to the provided return address

4. Refund Processing

Once we receive your return:

  • We will inspect the item within 2-3 business days
  • If approved, refund will be processed to your original payment method
  • Refunds typically appear within 5-7 business days
  • You will receive an email confirmation once refund is processed

5. Exchanges

We only replace items if they are defective or damaged. If you need to exchange an item, contact us at support@genzikart.com and send your item to our return address.

6. Shipping Costs

  • Return shipping costs are the customer's responsibility
  • If the return is due to our error (wrong or defective item), we will cover return shipping
  • Original shipping charges are non-refundable

7. Damaged or Defective Items

If you receive a damaged or defective item:

  • Contact us within 48 hours of delivery
  • Provide photos of the damaged item and packaging
  • We will arrange for replacement or full refund at no cost to you
